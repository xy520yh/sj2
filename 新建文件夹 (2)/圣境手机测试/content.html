New [Blessing System]

1. Charge Holy Tree to obtain [Blessing Fruit] of various qualities and [Blessing Coins]. Fruit of different qualities has a greater chance to be grown into [Blessing Crystals] of the matching quality;

5. [Blessing Coins] can be used to redeem rewards in the Blessing Shop.
